The Sports Valley

Podgrodzie, 75-903 Koszalin

The Sports Valley (Sportowa Dolina) is a wonderful place for rest, recreation, fun and sports. Both the oldest and the youngest spend their free time here in an interesting way. Sportowa Dolina is beautifully located, surrounded by greenery. There are amenities to play basketbool, do skateboarding and many others activities.
