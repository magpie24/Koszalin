Robin Hood Park

Racławicka 7, 75-900 Koszalin

Robin Hood Park is a place in the bosom of nature. The park is full of trees and is located right next to the river, which makes it an ideal place for walks, bike rides or rollerblading. For those interested, there are archery classes and exercises in an outdoor gym.
