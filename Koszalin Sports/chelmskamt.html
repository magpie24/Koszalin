Chełmska Mountain

Chełmska, Koszalin

Chełmska Mountain (Góra Chełmska) consists of hills surrounded by a picturesque forest. It is an ideal place for walks at any time of the year and for bike rides. At Góra Chełmska there is a rope park, a playground and an outdoor gym.
