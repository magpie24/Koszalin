The Water Valley

Sybiraków 7, 75-800 Koszalin

Water Valley (Wodna Dolina) is a perfect place for those who love sports activites in the nature. Here, you will find walking and running paths, outside gyms and tennis court. Also, you are free to take the bath in the lake! For the smaller visitors there is a fun playground!
